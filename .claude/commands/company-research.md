---
description: One-page research brief on a company for applications and interviews.
argument-hint: "<company>"
allowed-tools: Read, Write, WebSearch, WebFetch, Bash
---

Research the company in `$ARGUMENTS`.

Read `profile/profile.md` first for the user's stack and target region. Then
use WebSearch / WebFetch to build a one-page brief: business model, funding /
scale, core engineering stack, recent news (last 6 months), what they look for
at the user's experience level, office locations in the user's target region,
and compensation signal (Levels.fyi, Glassdoor, or the region's equivalent if
findable). Close with 2 to 3 specific hooks the user can use in a cover letter
or interview that tie their work and projects to the company's domain.
Save to `notes/research-{slug}-{today}.md` and print.
