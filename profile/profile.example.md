## Cover letter rules

- Length: 150 to 220 words, one page max.
- Open with: applying for [role] at [company], plus your current status in one clause.
- Cite one story from "Flagship stories" that matches the company's stack.
- Sign: `Best,` then `<Your Name>` on a new line.

## Writing style

- **No em dashes (—) in any generated text.** Use commas, periods, or semicolons.
  (Delete this rule if you do not care. It exists because AI text is often
  detected by em-dash overuse.)
- Banned words: passionate, innovative, thrilled, great fit, synergy, and other
  recruiter-bait. Be direct; write like a peer engineer.
